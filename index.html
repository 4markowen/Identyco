Identyco

our story

We've been making high quality signs in Nottingham and fitting them throughout the UK for 18 years. We've a printer/designer, engineer and electrician who each bring their own talents to design, build and install your project.

From industrial units, office signs and entire shop fronts we provide quality assurance from our years of experience, advice on getting the sign your business deserves and making sure, when needed, it meets legal requirements.

Click on the other tabs to learn more about Identyco

design

Measure twice, cut once. The preparation and design stage of your project lays the foundation to it's success. We provide a free no obligation quote for all your signage, CNC routing, Engraving, Joinery, and Woodworking needs.

By talking to us we can advise you on the cost of the project, getting the right impact from your sign, it's longevity and maintenance free reliability, the legal requirements it might need to meet, along with a providing drawings for the relevant planning authority.

To give you an idea of our work visit the gallery

build

We produce all our own sign trays, fret cut lettering, built up acrylic letters, illuminated signs, Edge lit LED signs. We'll also explain what those are if you're new to the world of signs, or if you're already in the trade we'll have a knowledgeable talk about what you need.

Take a look at some of the examples in our gallery.

install

The tricky bit. Which is where our years of experience in fitting (signs, shops fronts and interiors, etc) comes in. As it's at this step that looking after your project all the way through really counts. There may still be surprises, but by dealing directly with us we can solve them and make sure they are not unpleasant ones.

Safety is always paramount to us as well. We're International Powered Access Federation (IPAF) licenced operators, we've got a fully qualified electrician on the team, ensuring your signage is in safe hands.
