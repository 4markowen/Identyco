our story

We've been making high quality signs in Nottingham and fitting them throughout the UK for 18 years. In house professional staff include a printer/designer, engineer and electrician who each bring their own talents to design, build and install your project.

Covering all aspects of signage we provide quality assurance from our years of experience, advice on getting the sign your business deserves and making sure, when needed, it meets legal requirements.

CNC cutting

CNC Cutting
At the heart of our operation is a precision large format CNC cutter with a 3050 x 2050mm cutting area and automated 18 station tool changing facility. We work with one off projects through to production runs. We offer a complete service from drawing through to the finished product to both trade and private sectors. Our client base includes sign companies, designers, shop fitters, joiners, R&D designers, precision engineers, furniture manufacturers, boat builders, etc.

    We work with a variety of materials including

  • MDF
  • Laminate Faced MDF
  • Laminates and veneers
  • Hard Wood
  • Soft Wood
  • Laminates
  • Acrylics
  • Foam Board
  • Styrene
  • Aluminium Composite Panels
  • Engraving Laminates and Brass

We can offer an ‘in house’ drawing and design facility. We can work with pretty much any file format you care to send us We provide a nationwide delivery, feel free to get in touch with us today to discuss your needs.
